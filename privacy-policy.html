Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 15, 2024

Summary: Subtract is a privacy-first app. All your subscription data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or have access to your personal data.

Introduction

Subtract ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application Subtract (the "App").

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

All subscription information you enter into the App is stored locally on your device. We have no access to this data and cannot retrieve it.

Local Storage

The App stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Subscription names and details
  • Billing amounts and cycles
  • Reminder preferences
  • App settings

This data never leaves your device unless you choose to use iCloud Sync (Pro feature).

iCloud Sync (Pro Feature)

If you subscribe to Subtract Pro, you can optionally enable iCloud Sync. When enabled:

  • Your data is synced to your personal iCloud account
  • The sync uses Apple's iCloud infrastructure
  • Your data is stored in your own iCloud storage
  • We have no access to your iCloud data

iCloud Sync is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy and your iCloud terms of service.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For managing subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect anonymous transaction data. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store: For processing payments. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Analytics

We do not use any analytics or tracking services. We do not track how you use the App.

Notifications

The App may send you local notifications as reminders for subscription renewals or trial expirations. These notifications are generated locally on your device and are not sent through any external servers.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

Email: subtract.app@gmail.com

隐私政策

最后更新：2024年12月15日

摘要：Subtract 是一款隐私优先的应用。您的所有订阅数据都存储在您的设备本地。我们不会收集、存储或访问您的个人数据。

简介

Subtract（"我们"）致力于保护您的隐私。本隐私政策解释了当您使用我们的移动应用 Subtract（"本应用"）时，我们如何处理您的信息。

数据收集

我们不收集任何个人数据。

您在应用中输入的所有订阅信息都存储在您的设备本地。我们无法访问这些数据，也无法检索它。

本地存储

本应用在您的设备本地存储以下信息：

  • 订阅名称和详情
  • 账单金额和周期
  • 提醒偏好设置
  • 应用设置

除非您选择使用 iCloud 同步（Pro 功能），否则这些数据永远不会离开您的设备。

iCloud 同步（Pro 功能）

如果您订阅 Subtract Pro，您可以选择启用 iCloud 同步。启用后：

  • 您的数据将同步到您的个人 iCloud 账户
  • 同步使用 Apple 的 iCloud 基础设施
  • 您的数据存储在您自己的 iCloud 存储中
  • 我们无法访问您的 iCloud 数据

iCloud 同步受 Apple 隐私政策和您的 iCloud 服务条款约束。

第三方服务

本应用使用以下第三方服务：

  • RevenueCat：用于管理订阅。RevenueCat 可能收集匿名交易数据。请参阅 RevenueCat 隐私政策。
  • Apple App Store：用于处理支付。请参阅 Apple 隐私政策。

分析

我们不使用任何分析或跟踪服务。我们不会跟踪您如何使用本应用。

通知

本应用可能会向您发送本地通知，作为订阅续费或试用到期的提醒。这些通知在您的设备本地生成，不通过任何外部服务器发送。

儿童隐私

本应用不适用于 13 岁以下的儿童。我们不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。

政策变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。我们将通过在此页面上发布新的隐私政策并更新"最后更新"日期来通知您任何更改。
