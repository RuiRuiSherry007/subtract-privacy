Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a subscription?
Tap the + button on the home screen, then enter your subscription details or choose from popular services.
How do I enable iCloud Sync?
iCloud Sync is a Pro feature. Subscribe to Subtract Pro, then go to Settings → Subtract Pro to enable sync.
How do I restore my purchase?
Go to Settings → Subtract Pro → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
Is my data secure?
Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device. With Pro, sync uses your personal iCloud account - we never access your data.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to iPhone Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions → Subtract Pro → Cancel Subscription.
